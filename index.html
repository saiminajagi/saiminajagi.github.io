AB de Villiers sorry for very disappointing IPL season for RCB

Updated | May 26, 2017

Star batsman AB de Villiers has apologised for a disappointing season for Royal Challengers Bangalore at the 2018 Indian Premier League.

RCB finished sixth with 12 points in their kitty failing to qualify for the play-offs for the second year in a row. They have reached the final of the IPL three times but have never tasted victory.

It was yet another poor season by the franchise which started the T20 league as one of the favourites to win the trophy.

However, the team lacked consistency and barring skipper Virat Kohli and de Villiers no other batsman stepped up to the occasion.

RCB managed to win just 6 of their 14 matches.

Thanks to all the RCB fans in Bangalore, in all of India and around the world for supporting us throughout the 2018 season. You guys always right behind us and carried us a long way. Unfortunately, it was a very disappointing season for us and I am sorry for that," de Villiers said in a video posted by him on Instagram.
